Customer Help

Store policies regarding ordering, shipping, returns, and warranty info.

Order & Payment

We accept payments made via all major debit and credit cards.

Please write to us at support@mech.sg and quote your order number. Our support team will do their best to assist you.

Delivery & Shipping

All orders above SGD$100 will qualify for free delivery.

All orders will be delivered straight to your doorstep by our local couriers within 2-4 working days. Should there be any changes to your picked delivery dates and timings, we will contact you at least 1 working day in advance to notify you of the changes.

Unfortunately, once an order is shipped, we will be unable to make any changes or cancel it.

Warranty, Returns & Refunds

Our limited 1-year warranty covers all the components of your mechanical keyboard. For out-of-box defects, please email us at support@mech.sg with your order number and photos or videos of the issue at hand (if available).

All returns must be received by us within 7 days from the day you receive your items based on our records. Your return will be voided if it is not received by us during this time frame.
The products must also be returned in their original packaging and must be accompanied by a valid receipt or proof of purchase.
Returned items are subject to a condition check, after which we will notify you via email before proceeding with an exchange or refund. The refund processing period may vary between 5-7 working days from the reception of your returned items at our office.
